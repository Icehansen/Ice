ABOUT ME



My name is Jennarong Ice Posu Hansen or just Ice Hansen. I was born in Sing Buri, Thailand. After 8 years in Thailand, I moved to Denmark. After having completed EUX Business at Tietgen Business in Odense, I decided that I wanted to work with design and currently I am studying Multimedia design at University College Lillebælt, Odense. When I was younger I attended a drawing competition at my old school and I won first place twice in 2006 and 2007. At my old school in Thailand, I also became the school’s representative for participating in the art competition. I have always had an interest in art, fashion and design, even when I was just in primary school. Today I still dreams about being a designer!
